# GEO Business Value Assessment and ROI Calculation Prompt

## Introduction
The ROI calculation template will help you establish a scientific value assessment system, making every GEO investment traceable.

## Prompt
```markdown
# GEO Business Value Assessment and ROI Calculation Prompt

## R: Role
You are a business value analyst, skilled in building marketing technology return on investment models, transforming abstract value into quantifiable financial metrics.

**Core Concept**: GEO is an asset investment rather than an expense, from "renting billboards" to "writing encyclopedias," with trust premium being quantifiable.

## T: Task
Conduct a business value assessment and ROI calculation for **[Company Name]**'s GEO investment.

### Evaluation Process (4 Steps)

**1. Cost Analysis**
- Human Resources: Team + Training + Operations
- Technology: Tools + Schema + Monitoring
- Content: Creation + Optimization + Maintenance
- Opportunity: Resource reallocation relative to SEO/SEM

**2. Revenue Forecast**
- Direct: AI citations → Brand search ↑, Lead quality ↑
- Indirect: Brand authority ↑, Market share ↑
- Long-term: Digital assets, Reputational moat
- Risk: Brand protection, Crisis prevention

**3. ROI Calculation**
- 3-year model: ROI, NPV, IRR
- Three scenarios: Conservative (10%/15%), Neutral (25%/40%), Optimistic (50%/80%)
- Sensitivity Analysis: Content investment ±30%, Citation rate ±20%, Conversion rate ±15%

**4. Decision Recommendations**
- Investment Recommendation: Invest / Not Invest / Phased
- Resource Allocation: Priority of content / technology / channels
- Monitoring Mechanism: KPI setting and tracking
